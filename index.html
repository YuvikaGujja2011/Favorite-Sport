Tennis

Description

Tennis is a game where there is either one person on each side of the court or two people on each side of the court. There is singles which is played by 2 people and doubles which is played by 4 people. The main objective of the game is to hit the ball across the net with a racket. You have to hit the ball across the net and if the opposing player doesn't hit it back, you get a point.


Rules

In order to win a point, you need to hit the ball inside the white lines on the court. If you hit the ball inside the white lines and your opponent doesn't hit it back, you get a point. However, if a ball goes out of bounds, whoever hit the ball loses the point.


History

Tennis has evovled over three centuries and it comes from a ball game that was played in France around the 12th century. Although, at this time, you would hit the ball with the palm of your hand. In the 16th century, rackets were starting to be used. Tennis was originally named lawn tennis, which was invented in Birmingham, England. Now of course it is known as tennis.
